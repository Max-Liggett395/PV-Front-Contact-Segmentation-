## Architecture Notes

### FiLM Conditioning (feature/unet-segmentation)
- UNet supports optional FiLM (Feature-wise Linear Modulation) conditioning on magnification
- `num_magnifications=None` disables FiLM (backward compatible)
- FiLM layers applied after each encoder block + bottleneck (5 total), before max-pooling
- Magnification map: `data/magnification_map.json` (stem → KX float), generated by `scripts/extract_magnifications.py`
- 29 unique magnification categories across 116 images (0.75 KX to 13 KX)
- SEMDataset returns 3-tuple `(image, label, mag_id)` when magnification_map is provided
- Trainer auto-detects 2-tuple vs 3-tuple batches
- WeightedRandomSampler balances magnification distribution in training
- `test_model_parameter_count` was pre-existing failure (expects 7.8M but model has ~47M); not related to FiLM
